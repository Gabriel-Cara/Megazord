ABOUT THE TEAM

MEGAZORD 7563 is a robotics team founded in 2018 in the city of Jundiai, in Brazil. It has been competing at First Robotics Competition since 2019. Our team is sponsored by SESI and SENAI. These institutions provide all the necessary resources to develop all the required projects to be eligible for the tournament. Throughout the years, we have been spreading science and technology for everyone who does not have access and we aim to reach those who are not heard in the society.

THE REASON OF THE NAME MEGAZORD

The team has chosen this name because of Megazord from the series Mighty Morphin Power Rangers. On the show, this character shows up when all the Power Rangers combine their powers to build a giant mighty robot. In our team, all of our members have an ability that can help both in the mechanical and in the social fields. With all the members together, we become even stronger!
